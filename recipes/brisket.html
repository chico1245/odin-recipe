Brisket

Brisket is a Texas food staple that takes a few tries to get right. Follow the steps below and you'll save yourself the wasted meat.

Instructions

  1. Beef Brisket (8-12 lbs is ideal)
  2. Season with olive oil, salt, and pepper
  3. Smoke at 225F for 10 hours
  4. Wrap brisket in foil and crank heat to 275F for 1.5 hours
  5. Remove brisket when internal temperature is 197-202F and let is rest for 30min

Ingredients

  • Brisket trimmed 8-12lbs fresh
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper
