Fried Egg

Fried-egg is the first meal everyone should learn to cook.

Instructions

  1. Turn on stove to low/medium heat and add cooking pan
  2. Add butter or olive oil to the pan and let is heat for a 2 minutes
  3. Crack egg into a cup/bowl and add a little milk (makes it extra fluffly) and stir
  4. Add mixed egg/milk to pan and let is cook for 3-4 minutes and remove
  5. Remove brisket when internal temperature is 197-202F and let is rest for 30min

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Olive oil or butter
