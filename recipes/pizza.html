Pizza

Pizza is a food that brings joy and happiness to all kids.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 550F
  2. Precook dough with a light amount of sauce and sprinkled parmesan cheese
  3. Once the dough is browned and growing pull it out of the oven to add cheese and topings
  4. Once the dough looks cooked and cheese is melted pull the pizza out and enjoy

Ingredients

  • Fresh dough
  • Tomatoe sauce and cheese
  • Favorite topics
