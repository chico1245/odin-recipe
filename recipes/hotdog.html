Hotdog

Hotdog is an American delicacy that is sure to impress your guests.

Instructions

  1. Drive to Sonic's and order an American Hotdog
  2. Take the hotdogs home, remove from packaging and add to plate with a side of ketchup and mustard

Ingredients

  • Hotdog bun
  • Hotdog
  • Ketchup, mustard, and other preferred topings
